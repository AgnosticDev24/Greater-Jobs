Buildings and Facilities

Do you want to Build Your Community? Join Our Dynamic Team of Building and Facility Professionals at Salford City Council!

Do you want to add real value in your community?

If the answer is yes, then we may have the role for you!

About us

Salford City Council owns and operates a diverse portfolio of around 1,000 properties, ranging from Grade I listed heritage buildings and monuments to award-winning Grade A office spaces in the city’s heart.

Responsible for day-to-day maintenance, compliance, and refurbishments, the team ensures our estate remains operational and accessible for the community.

With an annual investment of £6M, Salford City Council delivers capital projects and sustainability initiatives, advancing our Net Zero goals.

We manage essential projects for schools, from roof replacements to designing modular buildings, enhancing the local educational infrastructure.

Be part of a talented team delivering high-quality property services and making a lasting impact on the local community.

Our current vacancies

Building Surveyor

Our Building Surveyors take initial concepts right through to completion for a variety of internal stakeholders. You will be key in maintaining quality whilst ensuring a value-for-money approach for both small and large projects. As a varied role you will be completing surveys, specifying and tendering works, whilst managing a programme of onsite works, therefore we’re looking for strong stakeholder and project management skills and someone who enjoys the freedom of working with their own initiative.

Water Hygiene Surveyor

A new role for Salford City Council which provides the opportunity for the right person to really make their mark on all things Water Safety.

We are looking for a water hygiene professional who will take the lead in ensuring that our water systems are safe, compliant with current legislation and our contractors are effectively managed.

You will have experience of reviewing and acting on water risk assessments, reviewing and writing policies, procedures and written schemes with the relevant qualifications to act as SCC’s responsible person for Water Hygiene.

Mechanical Surveyor

An opportunity to join a highly skilled and experienced Mechanical & Electrical Engineering team delivering repairs, maintenance, statutory compliance and capital works for a variety of mechanical systems.

Ideally you will have experience of delivering design through to completion of projects for HVAC systems including technologies such as heat pumps, air conditioning and commercial ventilation systems.

Senior Energy Officer

An opportunity to deliver real change through a range of carbon reduction initiatives to help Salford City Council continue our journey to Net-Zero.

We’re looking for someone with the skills and experience to complete energy audits and feasibility studies to a wide range of commercial buildings to ensure we are in strong provision to deliver a wide range of low-carbon initiatives

Over the past 3 years we have invested heavily in reducing our carbon footprint with the installation of solar farms, solar roof panels and installing heat pumps, therefore experience in project management of renewable and low-carbon technology projects would be a distinct advantage

Assets and Complaince Coordinator

A new role at the heart of what we do, the Assets and Compliance coordinator is a vital link within the team, from ensuring our properties and buildings are compliant to working with Surveyors on the prioritisation of future investment works.

We are currently continuing our journey to be a systems and data-led team, therefore it is vital that you bring knowledge of asset management / CAFM systems as well as being able to analyse a wide range of data to support us on this journey.

Our Generous Benefits

  • Holiday allowance up to 34 days
  • Paid sick leave

  • Family-related or emergency leave
